Brief

Think of a mobile native app (both iPhone and iPad) that captures supernatural practices. Of course, the app should be functional and the user has to believe in it.
No Harry Potter and the app of wisdom, base yourself on existing and credible practises! However, the authenticity is not that important for this assignment.

Concept

I knew from the beginning that I wanted to do something with plants. Soon I came with the idea from japanese tea ceremonies. The app has to be a medium that helps people with their needs. Depending on their biorythms, they get everyday a plant according to what they need that day. After they chose a plant, they can choose whether they want to use it in a tea or to grow the plant. They get an intro where they get prepared for the ceremony. With the force of spells and the plant, they will get the powers they need to feel better.

For the right look and feel from the app, I tried different styles and colors but the blue color with the white lines was my favourite from the beginning. I used some soft colors with soft drop shadows so the user gets in a calm mood. To highlight the Japanese theme, I translated a few words like the plants name and the menu items in Japanese.
I liked this assignment, you can check the clickable mockup.
