FjällRäven has noticed that they are still unknown to some of their target groups and so. Therefore, they want to set up a promotional action where a part of their gamma will be placed in the spotlight. We needed to create a fresh product website featuring a range of fascinating features. The user had to be well informed and driven to buy the product. We had to choose between 2 themes 'Back to school' and 'City Explorer'.

I chose for the 'City Explorer' collection which included 2 backpacks and a totebag. For this collection I focused on people who like to go on a city trip, both men as women.
After some research I found that these people wanted to know practical things about the bags, so I highlighted some features which are important for going on a citytrip. Like the safety of the tote bag, the flexibility, the weight...

Besides the practical things of the bags, I wanted to implement a fun thing for the custumors of these bags. My idea was to create a platform where customers can upload a picture with there bag and add the place where they have been with their bag. Other people can see on a map where Fjällräven bags have been, plus I putted the numbers of how far Fjällräven has travelled with his customers.
